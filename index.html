The bubble cursor: enhancing target acquisition by dynamic resizing of the cursor's activation area


Welcome to the Bubble Cursor Experiment.

This experiment will take no more than 30 minutes. To start each trial, click on the single green circle. During each trial in the experiment, you will see a circle green in color, that is your target circle. Kindly click on the target as fast as possible. Throughout the experiment, you will be informed about your progress at the bottom of the screen.

Thank you for your participation.
